Butter Sandwich

Description

Do I really need to put a description on a recipe page for a butter sandwich? It's a butter sandwich, lmao.

Ingredients

  • However many slices of bread you wish to consume
  • 1 stick of butter. What, 1 isn't enough?
  • Something to toast the bread with, like a toaster oven

Steps

  1. Turn the toaster on
  2. Put slices of bread into the oven
  3. Wait until desired crispiness
  4. Slice off 1 teaspoon of butter
  5. Put butter between slices of bread
  6. Eat
